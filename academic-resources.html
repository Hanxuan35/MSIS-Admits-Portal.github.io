Advising Support

As an admitted MSIS student, you’ll receive personalized guidance from academic advisors who are familiar with the MSIS curriculum and requirements. Whether you’re planning your class schedule, considering electives, or preparing for graduation, your advisor is here to help.

Scheduling an Advising Appointment

You can meet with your academic advisor in person or virtually through the Student Appointment Scheduler (SAS) system. We recommend scheduling early, especially during registration periods.
