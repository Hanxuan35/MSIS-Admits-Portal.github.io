Academic Support & Resources

Whether you need help with coursework, career prep, or navigating campus life, IU offers plenty of support systems. Below you’ll find essential academic resources available to MSIS students.

Get support with class planning, graduation requirements, and any academic issues you face. Learn more or schedule an appointment at MSIS Academic Advising.

The Kelley Peer Tutoring Program and IU Academic Support Centers provide assistance in courses like statistics, programming, and accounting. Visit Kelley Peer Tutoring or IU Academic Support.

Need help editing a report or preparing for a presentation? Check out the IU Writing Tutorial Services and the IU Public Speaking Lab.

IU provides access to powerful computing tools, including software like Tableau, SQL Server, and more through University Information Technology Services (UITS).

IU Libraries offer both physical and digital collections, plus expert librarians for help with research. Visit IU Libraries.
